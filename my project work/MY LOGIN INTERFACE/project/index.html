CHURCH DATABASE AND COMMUNICATION SYSTEM
Proceed

Practical Relevance:

Stores church member details (personal, family, and spiritual data). Tracks attendance and financial contributions. Facilitates communication through SMS, email, or notifications. Generates statistical and financial reports.

Academic Relevance:

The project will serve as a case study for applying database management, system analysis, and software development concepts to solve real-life problems in the field of Information Technology and Management Information Systems.

CDACS

CDACS is a computerized platform designed to automate the storage and retrieval of member information, enhance communication, and improve administrative efficiency in church operations.
